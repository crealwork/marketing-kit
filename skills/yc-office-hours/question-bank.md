# Question Bank

Organized by mode and topic. Select 4-6 questions per session based on stage and context.
Ask one at a time via AskUserQuestion. Never batch.

## Universal Questions (All Modes)

### Clarity & Vision
- "Explain what you do in one sentence. No jargon."
- "If you had to keep one thing and change everything else, what would you keep?"
- "What would you work on if you knew this was going to fail?"

### Evidence & Validation
- "What did you learn from your customers this week that you didn't know last week?"
- "Are customers paying, or just being nice? What happened when you asked for money?"
- "How much of your time is spent talking to customers vs. talking ABOUT customers?"

### Focus & Priority
- "What's the ONE number that matters most right now?"
- "What have you said no to recently? Was it hard?"
- "Look at your calendar from last week. What percentage was spent on your top priority?"
- "What's fake work at your company — feels productive but moves nothing?"

### Conviction & Founder Quality
- "What do you know about this market that nobody else knows?"
- "Are you 100% committed, or is this one of several options you're keeping open?"
- "What's the most embarrassing version you could ship this week?"

## IDEA Mode Questions

### Pre-Idea Stage
- "What problem did you personally experience that led to this?"
- "Sit next to your target customer for a day. Watch their screens. What's repetitive?"
- "What industry has large incumbents making lots of money without innovation?"

### Pre-Launch Stage
- "Have you launched? If not, why not? What are you afraid of?"
- "What's the minimum version that delivers value? Not ideal — minimum."
- "What feature are you planning to build before launch that you could cut entirely?"
- "Could you deliver the core value with Google Sheets, a phone number, and email?"
- "Who are the 5 people with such a hair-on-fire problem they'd use your janky MVP?"

### Post-Launch Stage
- "After your last launch, what did you learn? What did you change?"
- "Out of your last 100 signups, how many have the problem you're solving? Can you name them?"
- "What percentage of your work is automated vs manual? What was it 3 months ago?"
- "If you turned off all paid acquisition today, what would growth look like?"

### Pivot Assessment
- "Do you still believe this will work? Be honest."
- "What's your main KPI? Can you tell me without hesitating?"
- "Have you asked 'would you pay for this?' What happened?"
- "Are you pivoting because the idea failed, or because you got bored?"
- "What did you learn from each previous attempt? List concrete learnings."

### AI-Era Specific
- "How are you using LLMs internally right now, regardless of product?"
- "If models become 2x better in 12 months, does your product become irrelevant or 10x better?"
- "What's the specific manual workflow your AI replaces? Describe it step by step."
- "What percentage of your service delivery is automated? What's the trend?"

## GROWTH Mode Questions

### Product-Market Fit
- "If your product disappeared tomorrow, would customers notice? Would they care?"
- "What's your net dollar retention? If you don't know, that's a problem."
- "Describe your best customer in 2 sentences. Now describe your worst. What's different?"

### Scaling Readiness
- "What breaks first at 10x your current volume?"
- "What's your revenue per employee? Is it going up or down?"
- "Are you hiring because things are breaking, or because you have money to spend?"
- "What function is most overwhelmed right now?"

### Spending & Runway
- "How many months of runway at current burn? What if you cut all non-essentials?"
- "Are you spending money to feel like a real company, or to find PMF?"
- "When was the last time you sent an investor update? What did it say about burn?"
- "Before hiring any non-engineering role: have you personally done that job for 3+ months?"

### Go-To-Market
- "Who are you selling to and how do you get their attention? One sentence each."
- "Are your buyers empowered to make the purchase decision, or stuck in committee?"
- "Have you figured out the sales playbook yourself before hiring someone to run it?"
- "What's your ratio of technical to non-technical people? Is it above 30% technical?"

## CAMPAIGN Mode Questions

### Strategy & Positioning
- "What is the single message this campaign communicates? Not two — one."
- "Does this campaign promise match the actual product experience?"
- "What would a competitor say about this campaign? How would they attack it?"
- "Is this campaign content first, marketing second? Would the target read it without the CTA?"

### Audience
- "Name one real person this campaign targets. What's their title? What gets them fired?"
- "Where does this person already spend time online? Is that where this campaign lives?"
- "What's the status quo for this person? What are they doing today without you?"
- "Are you targeting early adopters, or trying to convince the mainstream? Pick one."

### Channel & Distribution
- "Is this the channel where the audience already spends time, or where you wish they did?"
- "What happens if you turn off ads for 2 weeks? If growth dies, you have no real channel."
- "Have you tried manual, personal outreach before scaling with automation?"
- "What did you learn from your last 10 manual outreach attempts?"

### Messaging & Creative
- "Read the headline out loud. Does it sound like a human wrote it, or a committee?"
- "What's the one thing you want the reader to DO after seeing this? Is it obvious?"
- "Is this copy speaking to the customer's problem, or bragging about your features?"
- "Would this email/ad make you stop scrolling if you saw it? Why or why not?"

### Measurement
- "What's the one metric that tells you this campaign worked?"
- "What's your expected cost per acquisition? How does that compare to customer LTV?"
- "How will you know within 48 hours if this campaign is working?"
- "What's the minimum sample size before you make a decision on this campaign?"

## Action Templates

### IDEA Mode Actions
- "Talk to 5 potential customers this week. Ask if they'd pay $X. Report back with exact quotes."
- "Build the jankiest version that delivers core value. Ship by Friday. No user accounts needed."
- "Write the one-sentence pitch and send it to 10 people. Track: how many ask a follow-up question?"
- "Find one person with the hair-on-fire problem. Solve it manually. Document what you learned."
- "Sit next to your target customer for 4 hours. Watch their screen. List every repetitive task."

### GROWTH Mode Actions
- "Turn off all paid ads for 2 weeks. Measure organic growth. Report the number."
- "Set one KPI. Put it on a dashboard everyone sees. Cut one activity not moving it."
- "Audit your calendar: what % of time went to your top priority? Write the number down."
- "Send an investor update this week with 3 numbers: runway, burn, revenue. No narrative."
- "Respond to every customer support ticket within 4 hours for one week. Track what you learn."

### CAMPAIGN Mode Actions
- "Send this campaign to 10 real prospects manually. Track reply rate before scaling."
- "Cut the campaign to 1 message, 1 CTA, 1 audience. Test that version first."
- "Write the campaign as if you had zero budget. What's the manual version?"
- "A/B test the subject line with 100 recipients before sending to the full list."
- "Ask 3 people in the target audience to read the campaign and tell you what it's about. If they can't, rewrite."
